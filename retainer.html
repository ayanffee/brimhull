How It Works

A clear, structured process from onboarding to ongoing support.

1

Onboarding

We start by getting oriented with your current operations and immediate goals. We pinpoint areas where your business is exposed and identify the legal structures required to support your goals. By the end of this stage, we transition from “getting to know you” into a clear action plan of what your business needs right now to move forward securely.

2

Execution

Using the action plan as our guide, we begin implementation. This is where we do the heavy lifting: drafting bespoke contracts, securing your IP ownership, and negotiating terms that protect your interests. We deliver on the precision we promised by building custom legal structures that address your immediate needs and support your long-term growth.

3

Maintenance

Once those legal structures are built, we stay in the room. Through monthly check-ins and active monitoring, we provide the ongoing support needed to keep your business compliant. We stay in the loop of your operations, giving you the security of a legal desk without the overhead of a full-time hire.
