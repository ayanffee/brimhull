FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What does 'fixed fee' mean? +
You'll always know the cost upfront. No hourly billing, no surprise invoices. The price you see is the price you pay. This gives you complete transparency and helps you budget accurately for your legal needs.
How do I get started? +
Simply fill out our contact form or select a service from our pricing menu. We'll schedule a brief consultation to understand your needs and get to work. Most clients hear back within 24 hours of reaching out.
Do you work with artists outside Nigeria? +
Yes. While we're based in Nigeria, we work with creatives across Africa and internationally on IP and commercial matters. Distance is no barrier when it comes to protecting your creative work.
How quickly can I get my documents? +
Most standard agreements are delivered within 3-5 business days. Rush delivery is available for urgent matters. We understand that creative projects move fast, and we're set up to keep pace.
Can I pay online? +
We're currently setting up online payments. For now, select a service and we'll send you payment details directly. Online payment options will be available very soon.
What if I need something not listed? +
Reach out through our contact form. We handle custom legal work regularly and will provide a quote within 24 hours. Your unique needs matter, and we're happy to discuss custom solutions.
