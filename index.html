Meet the Founder

Tobechi Nwosu

Principal Partner, Brim & Hull Legal Practitioners

Tobechi Nwosu is a legal practitioner with a singular mission: to build the legal infrastructure that Africa's creative economy has been missing. Trained in intellectual property and commercial law, she saw firsthand how creators, from musicians and filmmakers to artists and entrepreneurs, were underserved by traditional law firms that didn't understand their world.

That's why she founded Brim & Hull. A firm that speaks the language of creators. One that replaces opaque billable hours with transparent fixed fees. One that treats your catalogue, your brand, and your vision with the same rigour that corporate law affords billion-dollar mergers.

Your art deserves the same legal protection that multinational deals get. That's what we're here to build.
