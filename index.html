About Brim & Hull

The law firm built for Africa's most ambitious creators

Brim & Hull builds the legal infrastructure creators and entrepreneurs need for global scale. From rights clearance to strategic advisory and deal-making, we ensure you're always investment-ready, protected, and primed for the world stage. Meet the Director

Tobechi Nwosu

Director, Brim and Hull

Tobechi Nwosu is a legal practitioner with a singular mission: to build the legal infrastructure that Africa's creative economy has been missing. Trained in intellectual property and commercial law, she saw firsthand how creators — from musicians and filmmakers to artists and entrepreneurs — were underserved by traditional law firms that didn't understand their world.

That's why she built Brim and Hull. A firm that speaks the language of creators. One that replaces opaque billable hours with transparent fixed fees. One that treats your catalogue, your brand, and your vision with the same rigour that corporate law affords billion-dollar mergers.

Your art deserves the same level of legal protection as any Fortune 500 company. That's not aspirational — that's what we deliver.
